eague of Legos

How to use

League of Legends is a 5v5 team game. League of Legos takes a look at all professional games played between the 2015 and 2018 seasons.

Each player acrues gold throughout the game and League of Legos allows you to look at the individual stats for each player throughout each game.

If you don't know any specific head to head matches, you can always search with just one team! A classic match up is C9 vs TSM. Do note: C9 vs TSM pulls up different games from TSM vs C9 because games are played on their respective blue/red sides.
